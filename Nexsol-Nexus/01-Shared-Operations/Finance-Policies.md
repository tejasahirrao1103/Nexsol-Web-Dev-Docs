# Finance & Invoicing Policies: Nexsol Nexus

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The 3-day refund policy is strict. Resources are locked within 72 hours of payment receipt to ensure sub-agent and server availability.

**Objective:** Financial clarity and resource protection for all agency projects.

---

## 1. Standard Payment Milestones
- **40% Advance:** Non-refundable after 3 days. Secures server space and engineering talent.
- **40% Beta Release:** Triggered upon functional demo on staging environment.
- **20% Deployment:** Triggered upon domain pointing and final handover.

## 2. Invoicing Procedure
1. Sales Lead issues Quote -> Client Approves.
2. Finance Ops issues Professional GST-Compliant Invoice.
3. Work begins **only** after funds are cleared in the bank.

## 3. Expense & Budgeting
- No department can spend >₹1,000 on external tools/assets without Lead approval.
- All SaaS subscriptions (Vercel, OpenAI, Shopify) must be tracked in the Central Finance Sheet.

## 4. Late Payment Protocol
- **Reminder 1:** 2 days past due.
- **Reminder 2:** 5 days past due (Work on staging is paused).
- **Reminder 3:** 7 days past due (Interest of 2% added to final invoice).
