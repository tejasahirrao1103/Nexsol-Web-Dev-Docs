# Operations SOP: Client Communication & Expectation Management

**Objective:** Maintaining high trust through transparency and predictable touchpoints.
**Core Principle:** "High Detail, Low Frequency."

---

## 1. The Onboarding Kick-off (Day 1)
*   **Action:** Send the "Welcome to Nexsol Nexus" email.
*   **Checklist:**
    *   [ ] Request DNS Access (GoDaddy/Cloudflare).
    *   [ ] Request Marketplace/Shopify Credentials.
    *   [ ] Request Brand Assets (Logo, Colors, High-res product images).
    *   [ ] Introduce their primary point of contact (Operations Lead).

---

## 2. The "Nexsol Friday Pulse" (Weekly Update)
*   **Format:** 5-minute Loom screen-record.
*   **Structure:**
    1.  **Achievements:** What was built this week (Show, don't tell).
    2.  **Obstacles:** Any technical blockers encountered.
    3.  **Next Sprint:** What is planned for Monday-Wednesday.
    4.  **Action Item:** "I specifically need [X] from you by Monday."

---

## 3. Handling Scope Creep (The "Red Flag" Pivot)
When a client says: *"Can you also add a blog section?"* (if not in SOW).
*   **Response:** "That sounds like a great addition to your digital authority. Currently, we are focused on the core marketplace-to-brand integration as per our SOW. I've noted this blog request—let's complete the launch first, and then we can issue a 'Growth Add-on' SOW for the blog section."
*   **Why:** Protects the 21-day timeline.

---

## 4. The Beta Review Meeting (Day 12-14)
*   **Goal:** Final sign-off for Phase 2.
*   **Deliverable:** A staging link (e.g., `client-beta.nexsol.dev`).
*   **Script:** "We have achieved the sub-2s performance target. The AI is now trained on your inventory. Please review the checkout flow today so we can move to final deployment."

---

## 5. Post-Launch Handover
*   **Day 21:** Send the "Handoff Vault" (Login details, Training recording, Final Invoice).
*   **Post-Launch Survey:** Send the 1-minute "How did we do?" form.
*   **Reference Request:** If the score is 9/10+, request a LinkedIn recommendation for Tejas/Kanhaiya.
