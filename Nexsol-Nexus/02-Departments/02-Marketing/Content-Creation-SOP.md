# Marketing SOP: Content Creation & Distribution

**Objective:** Maintaining a consistent flood of "Technical Authority" content to attract high-ticket SME clients.
**Core Messaging:** "Next-Gen ROI for Modern Indian Brands."

---

## 1. Weekly Content Calendar (The 3-3-1 Model)
To maintain top-of-mind awareness without burnout:

*   **3 LinkedIn Posts:**
    *   *Post 1:* High-level strategy (e.g., "The Marketplace Commission Trap").
    *   *Post 2:* Technical Flex (e.g., "How we achieved sub-2s loads with Next.js").
    *   *Post 3:* Personal/Company Culture (e.g., "Founders' Vision for Indian Tech").
*   **3 WhatsApp Stutus Updates:**
    *   *Story 1:* Live Demo snippet (AI Agent talking).
    *   *Story 2:* Client Win (New site launch).
    *   *Story 3:* FAQ of the Week (Technical answer).
*   **1 Long-form Blog/Case Study:**
    *   Deep dive into a specific problem solved (e.g., "Integrating ONDC for a Nashik Distributor").

---

## 2. Blog Writing Standard (SEO & Authority)
Every Nexsol blog must follow this structure to ensure conversion:

1.  **The Hook:** Start with a specific rupee loss (e.g., "Losing 20% to Amazon?").
2.  **The Solution:** Introduce the Nexsol technical approach (AI/Next.js).
3.  **The Proof:** Show a code snippet, a PageSpeed score, or a Mermaid diagram.
4.  **The Comparison:** Nexsol (Asset) vs. Generic (Liability).
5.  **The CTA:** Lead them to the **ROI Audit Form.**

---

## 3. Visual Content Guidelines
*   **Mockups:** Use the Nexsol "Dark Premium" mockup frame for all site showcases.
*   **Diagrams:** Use Mermaid.js or professional flowcharts to explain complex concepts (e.g., RAG logic).
*   **Fonts:** Bold `Outfit` for headlines; `Inter` for body.
*   **Colors:** Forced high-contrast Slate 900 base with Blue 500 accents.

---

## 4. Repurposing Strategy
Don't write twice. Follow the "Atomic Content" loop:
1.  **Atom:** 1 Long-form Blog (Technical).
2.  **Splinter:** Extract 3 LinkedIn posts from the blog logic.
3.  **Visual:** Create 1 carousel for Instagram/WhatsApp from the blog's diagrams.
4.  **Short:** Record a 60-second Loom explaining the core concept of the blog.

---

## 5. Distribution Checklist
*   [ ] Post link to LinkedIn (Native post, link in comments).
*   [ ] Share screenshot to WhatsApp Status.
*   [ ] Update the internal "Authority Vault" (Google Drive).
*   [ ] Feed the technical data back to the AI Sales Agent for training.
