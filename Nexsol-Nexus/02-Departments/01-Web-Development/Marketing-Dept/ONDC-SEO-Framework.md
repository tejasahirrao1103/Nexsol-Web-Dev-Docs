# Marketing SOP: ONDC-Specific SEO Framework

**Objective:** Dominating search results for Indian SMEs looking to transition from marketplaces to direct ownership.

---

## 1. Targeted Keyword Clusters
Focus on "Intent-Rich" long-tail keywords that signal a business ready to invest:

*   **Platform Neutrality:** "How to join ONDC without a mediator."
*   **Technical Integration:** "Nexsol ONDC integration for electronics."
*   **Profit Optimization:** "Calculating ONDC profit vs Amazon commissions."
*   **Localized Authority:** "Best web development agency for ONDC in Maharashtra."

---

## 2. On-Page Structure for SME Pages
When building/marketing a client's ONDC-ready site, ensure these SEO elements are present:

1.  **Product Schema:** JSON-LD for "Store" and "Offer" types to ensure Google shows price and availability in search.
2.  **ONDC Badge:** Visual and technical proof of integration to build trust.
3.  **Local Landing Pages:** If the client has physical outlets, create `/[city]/[niche]` routes to capture local ONDC search traffic.

---

## 3. Backlink & Authority Building
Authority isn't just code; it's external validation.
*   **PR:** Reach out to local business news portals in Nashik/Pune to feature "SME Digital Transformation" stories.
*   **Case Studies:** Publish technical case studies on Medium/LinkedIn tagging ONDC stakeholders.
*   **Guest Posting:** Targeted guest posts on SME-focused finance blogs (e.g., ClearTax, Razorpay blogs).

---

## 4. The "ONDC ROI" Calculator (Lead Magnet)
*   **The Concept:** A simple web toll where a user enters their Amazon/Flipkart sales volume and commission rate.
*   **The Output:** "You are losing ₹X every year. We can save you ₹Y."
*   **The Conversion:** Require an email/phone number to see the full "Sovereignty Report."

---

## 5. Metadata Excellence
*   **Title Tags:** Max 60 characters. Must include [City] and [ROI metric].
*   **Meta Description:** Focus on "Direct Ownership" and "Zero Commissions."
*   **Image Alt Text:** For every product image, include "Custom SME Brand store for [Product Category]."
