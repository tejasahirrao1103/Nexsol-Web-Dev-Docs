# Sales SOP: High-Impact Lead Qualification Script

**Topic:** Primary Discovery Call Framework (The 10-Minute Audit).
**Objective:** Effectively qualify leads into Static (₹7k), E-com (₹15k), or Enterprise (Value-Based) tiers while establishing technical authority.

---

## 1. The Opening (The "Pattern Interrupt")
*Goal: Move from "Salesman" to "Consultant" in 30 seconds.*

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Nexsol Infotech. I'm calling because we're conducting technical audits for [Niche: e.g., electronics sellers in Maharashtra]. I noticed your store on ONDC/Amazon—your products look great, but I noticed a significant gap in your independent brand authority. Do you have 2 minutes to hear how we help sellers reclaim their commissions?"

---

## 2. The "SME Pain" Framework (Primary Questions)
Use these to identify the "Value Gap."

### A. The Resource Drain
*   "How many manual calls/WhatsApp messages do you have to answer every day for simple questions like 'Where is my order' or 'What is the price'?"
*   *Note:* If >20 calls, they are a prime candidate for **AI Support Automation (Enterprise Tier).**

### B. The Marketplace Tax
*   "On a ₹1,000 sale, how much are you actually taking home after marketplace commissions and shipping fees?"
*   *Note:* If they hesitate, offer: "Most our clients lose 25%. We build systems where you keep 100%."

### C. The Scalability Wall
*   "If you got 500 orders today, would your current website/manual process crash or could you fulfill them in one click?"
*   *Note:* Identifying bottlenecks in fulfillment leads to **Operations Automation sales.**

---

## 3. Tier Placement Logic
*Based on their answers, pivot to the correct price anchor:*

| Response | Tier | Anchor Price | Next Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "I just need a professional look." | **Static** | ₹7,000 | Schedule Design Review |
| "I want to sell on ONDC & my site." | **E-commerce** | ₹15,000 | Schedule Inventory Sync Demo |
| "I'm overwhelmed by support/leads." | **Enterprise** | Value-Based | Schedule AI Audit |

---

## 4. The Closing Ask (Setting the Meeting)
"Based on what you've told me, you're losing approximately [Calculated Amount] per month in marketplace leakage. I want to schedule a 15-minute Technical Strategy session with our Tech Lead, Kanhaiya, to show you the exact blueprint for your D2C growth. Does Thursday at 11 AM work?"

---

## 5. Script Guardrails
*   **DO NOT** talk about 'Wordpress' or 'Coding' unless asked.
*   **DO** talk about 'Conversion Lift' and 'Commission Avoidance.'
*   **DO** mention ONDC integration as a standard Nexsol feature.
