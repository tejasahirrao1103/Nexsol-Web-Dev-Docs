# Transformation SOP: The Digital Audit Protocol

**Objective:** Providing the "Entry Point" for SMEs by identifying their technical and ROI gaps.
**Target:** Business owners still relying on manual processes or slow websites.

---

## 1. The Audit Infrastructure
We audit three distinct layers of a client's business:

1.  **Performance Layer:** Speed, SEO, and Mobile-friendliness.
2.  **Conversion Layer:** Checkout friction, UI/UX aesthetics, and Trust signals.
3.  **Operation Layer:** Manual lead handling, CRM usage, and inventory sync speed.

---

## 2. Tools for Analysis
*   **PageSpeed Insights:** For the "Zero-Downtime" benchmark.
*   **BuiltWith:** To identify outdated tech (WordPress/Old PHP).
*   **Nexsol ROI Script:** Internal calculator for marketplace commission leakage.
*   **Hotjar (Optional):** If they have an existing site, for heatmaps.

---

## 3. The "Gap Analysis" Report (Deliverable)
Every Transformation consultant must produce a 3-page PDF detailing:
*   **Current State:** "Your site loads in 6.2s—you are losing 45% of mobile traffic."
*   **The Nexsol Vision:** "With Next.js, we get you to 1.8s, salvaging that traffic."
*   **ROI Forecast:** "By eliminating manual lead scoring via our AI agent, you save 20 hours/week."

---

## 4. The Transition Roadmap
1.  **Phase 0 (Discovery):** This Audit.
2.  **Phase 1 (Brand):** Rebuilding the technical foundation (Next.js/React).
3.  **Phase 2 (Automation):** Integrating AI Agents and n8n workflows.
4.  **Phase 3 (Scale):** ONDC onboarding and paid authority loops.

---

## 5. Conversion to Sales
The Audit is free, but the "Blueprints" are proprietary. If the client wants the technical specs to fix their gaps, they must sign the **Phase 1 Exploration SOW**.
