# Sales SOP: CRM Discipline & Pipeline Movement

**Objective:** Ensuring no lead falls through the cracks and data-driven sales forecasting.
**Tools:** [Specific CRM - e.g., HubSpot / Pipedrive / Custom Nexsol Airtable].

---

## 1. The Nexsol Sales Pipeline Stages

Each lead must move through these stages in order. Never skip a status.

| Stage | Action Required | Expected Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Lead Inbound** | Assign owner within 4 hours. | Qualification Attempt scheduled. |
| **2. Cold Outreach** | Start the 6-Day ROI Pulse Loop. | Response received? (Yes/No). |
| **3. Qualified** | Discovery Script completed + Budget anchored. | Scheduled Tech Strategy Session. |
| **4. Technical Audit** | Tech Lead (Kanhaiya) presents ROI Blueprint. | Proposal/SOW Issued. |
| **5. Negotiation** | Handling objections + Term adjustments. | 40% Advance Invoice Sent. |
| **6. Closed Won** | Payment confirmed + Handover to Ops. | Kick-off call scheduled. |
| **7. Closed Lost** | Log "Lost Reason" + Move to Retargeting. | Future nurture loop active. |

---

## 2. Lead Entry Standards
**Mandatory Fields for every Lead:**
*   **Business Niche:** (e.g., Electronics, Fashion, Grocery).
*   **Current Margin Loss:** (Estimate of marketplace commissions).
*   **Tech Stack:** (Current site? Shopify? None?).
*   **Decision Maker:** (Owner? Marketing Head?).
*   **Pain Point:** (e.g., "Too many support calls", "Low SEO rank").

---

## 3. The "No Sleep" Follow-up Rule
If a lead is in **Stage 2 (Outreach)**:
*   Follow the automated 6-day sequence.
*   Log every attempt (Email, WhatsApp, Call).
*   If no response after Day 6, move to "Stale" and assign to the 30-day "Low Touch" nurture loop.

---

## 4. Sales-to-Ops Handover (The "Golden Move")
When a deal is marked **Closed Won**, the Sales lead must provide the following to the Ops Team:
1.  **Client Vision Document:** What is the one thing they care about most? (Speed/AI/ONDC).
2.  **Confirmed SOW:** What features were customized?
3.  **Payment Proof:** Verification of the 40% lock-in fee.

---

## 5. Performance Benchmarks
*   **L2D (Lead to Discovery):** 30% conversion goal.
*   **D2C (Discovery to Close):** 15% conversion goal.
*   **Average Cycle:** 14-21 days from Lead Inbound to Payment.
